Key Features

Lossless Compression

Our algorithm uses lossless compression to reduce the size of your images without losing any information. This ensures that your images look great while taking up less space on your website.

Batch Processing

You can upload multiple images at once and our algorithm will compress them all in one go. This saves you time and effort, and makes it easy to compress large numbers of images at once.

Customizable Settings

Our algorithm lets you customize the compression settings to suit your needs. You can choose the level of compression to apply, adjust the image dimensions, and choose between different image formats such as JPEG, PNG, and WebP. This flexibility allows you to strike a balance between image quality and file size, so you can achieve the best possible results for your specific use case.
